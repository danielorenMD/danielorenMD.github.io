I'm a physician-scientist in cardiology at Columbia. My clinical and research work centers on advanced heart failure and heart transplantation — with a particular focus on molecular diagnostics for allograft surveillance, mechanical circulatory support, and inflammatory cardiomyopathies. This site is where I share my research and write about cardiovascular science.
